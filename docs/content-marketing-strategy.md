## 1. Executive Summary

Nova AI Suite operates in the recruitment marketing technology space -- a $3.4B market growing at 18% CAGR. Despite having a technically superior product (33 AI tools, 24 LLM providers, real-time labor market data), we have near-zero organic visibility. No blog. No SEO footprint. No content engine.

This is both a problem and an opportunity. Our competitors (Appcast, PandoLogic, Recruitics, Joveo's enterprise product) have established content programs, but most produce generic, surface-level content. None leverage live AI + real labor market data to produce genuinely useful, data-driven content.

**The strategy:** Build a content engine that positions Nova AI Suite as the authoritative source for recruitment media planning intelligence. Every piece of content will be data-backed, actionable, and designed to capture high-intent search traffic from TA leaders actively evaluating tools or solving media planning problems.

**12-month targets:**
- 50,000 monthly organic visitors (from 0)
- 500 marketing-qualified leads from content
- Page 1 rankings for 25+ target keywords
- 200+ quality backlinks
- Establish Nova as a recognized thought leader in recruitment media planning

## 12. Content Governance

### 12.1 Brand Voice Guidelines

| Attribute | Do | Don't |
|---|---|---|
| **Tone** | Authoritative, data-driven, helpful | Salesy, condescending, jargon-heavy |
| **Perspective** | "We analyzed the data and here's what we found" | "Our amazing product does X" |
| **Data usage** | Always cite source, link to methodology | Round numbers without source, cherry-pick |
| **Competitor mentions** | Factual, respectful, focus on differentiation | Negative, dismissive, unverified claims |
| **CTA approach** | Contextual, value-first ("Get the full report") | Aggressive ("BUY NOW"), every paragraph |
| **Complexity** | Explain jargon on first use, use analogies | Assume reader knows abbreviations |

### 12.2 Content Update Policy

| Content Type | Update Frequency | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Benchmark reports | Quarterly | New data available from APIs |
| Evergreen guides | Every 6 months | SEO ranking changes, industry shifts |
| Comparison posts | Every 3 months | Product changes, pricing updates |
| Pillar pages | Annually | Major strategy shift or keyword changes |
| Templates/tools | As needed | User feedback, product updates |

### 12.3 Legal & Compliance

| Requirement | Policy |
|---|---|
| **Data attribution** | All statistics must cite primary source with link |
| **Competitor claims** | Must be verifiable from public sources (pricing pages, G2 reviews) |
| **AI disclosure** | All AI-assisted content must include disclosure: "Research assisted by Nova AI. Edited and verified by [human editor name]." |
| **Sponsored content** | Must be clearly labeled per FTC guidelines |
| **Image licensing** | Only use licensed, original, or properly attributed images |
| **Privacy** | No customer names/data without written consent |

### 12.4 Content Retirement Process

1. If a post has < 100 organic sessions/month after 6 months, evaluate for refresh or retirement
2. If data is more than 12 months old and cannot be updated, add a disclaimer banner
3. Retired content is 301-redirected to the most relevant active page (never deleted)
4. Quarterly content audit identifies candidates for refresh, merge, or retirement
