Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use Nova AI Suite: your name and email address (optional, only if you choose to provide them), and campaign or job data that you enter into our tools for the purpose of generating media plans and recruitment intelligence reports.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for generating media plans, recruitment intelligence reports, and AI-powered recommendations within the platform. We also use aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to improve AI recommendation quality across the suite.

3. Data Retention

Campaign data is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently on our servers. API cache data is cleared automatically every 24 hours. We do not maintain long-term databases of your campaign inputs or generated outputs.

4. Third-Party Services

We use external APIs for market data enrichment, including the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED), and other publicly available data sources. For product analytics and error monitoring, we use PostHog and Sentry. No personal data is shared with these services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any data associated with your usage. Since we do not store campaign data permanently, most data is already transient. For any data-related requests, please contact us using the information below.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please email shubhamsingh@Joveo.com.
